Ammoride was founded by former members of the military. Our military training has provided in-hand testing of weapon systems and tactical gear.

Ammoride’s specialty is providing tactical gear solutions and integrating next-generation technology with existing systems and equipment, to our clients. These include the US Government, the federal government, agencies, and other prospective clients. We ensure you will complete the task while maintaining public security, being on-time and on-target.

Our team secures the success of your projects with the latest gear and technologies on the market.

We provide: Firearms, Ammunition, Safety Gear, Tactical Gear, Medical Gear, and Aircraft Parts.

Ammoride has a continuous improvement model to keep our team up-to-date with the latest gear and technologies on the market. Therefore, we are always looking for alternatives and better technical solutions, as well as being a product integrator.
